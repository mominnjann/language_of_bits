In This Markdown file I will define important terms related to the language of bits

___

<MARK>*BIT*</MARK> : A variable that can have two values : 0 and 1 .

<MARK>*BYTE*</MARK> : A sequence of 8 bits .

<MARK>*BOOLEAN VARIABLE*</MARK> : A variable that can take only two values - 0 or 1 .

<MARK>*BOOLEAN ALGEBRA*</MARK> : An algebra consisting of boolean variables and some special operators defined on them.
